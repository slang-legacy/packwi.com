Your International Shipping Expert

Whether you need to ship a single item by air or a full container load via ocean, The Packaging Store of The Greater Milwaukee, WI Area can navigate the maze of paperwork and complex customs regulations, while providing competitive international rates.
The Packaging Store has more than 25 years of experience shipping internationally through our U.S. and Canadian network of locations. We also have the packaging expertise to protect your fragile, large and valuable goods against harsh overseas shipping environments. Get your international shipment there with care.

The Packaging Store provides the following services

  • International Air Freight for your time sensitive needs.
  • Less than container load (LCL) consolidation to save you money.
  • Full Container Loads for your larger shipments.
  • ISPM-15 heat-treated wood crates to meet international regulations.
  • Declared value coverage up to any value based on your individual shipment needs.
  • Door-to-Door, Door-to-Port or Port-to-Port options.
  • Residential or business delivery.
  • International Forms and Documentation Management
